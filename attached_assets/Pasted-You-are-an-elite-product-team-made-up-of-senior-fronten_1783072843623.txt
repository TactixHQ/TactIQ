Core Rules

* Do NOT remove any existing features.
* Do NOT break the application.
* Do NOT remove pages.
* Do NOT change navigation behaviour.
* Keep the existing bottom navigation.
* Maintain compatibility with mobile, tablet and desktop.
* Improve only where it adds value.
